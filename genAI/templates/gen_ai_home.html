About Gen AI

Generative AI refers to a class of artificial intelligence technologies that are used to generate new content, such as images, text, audio, and video, that is similar to content that was used to train the AI model. Unlike traditional AI systems that are designed to perform specific tasks based on predefined rules, generative AI models are capable of creating new content that has not been explicitly programmed into them.
