Batch Data Processing

Batch processing involves processing large volumes of data at once in batches or groups.

Advantages
  • Data is collected over a period of time (e.g., hours, days, or weeks).
  • Processed offline,once a sufficient amount of data is accumulated.
  • Completion time is not critical; it can take as long as needed.
  • Provides an economical and straightforward processing method for business applications.
  • Data is often sorted before processing.

Real-time Data Processing

It is essential for quick responses and handling a large number of events within tight deadlines.

Advantages
  • Real-time processing systems are very fast and responsive.
  • Process data immediately or with minimal delay as it is generated or ingested.
  • Quick transaction handling
  • Supplies immediate responses.
  • Reduction in Data Loss & Facilitated Data Recovery

Benefits

Achieve sustainable and purpose-driven value.

  • Significant results with smaller and simpler datasets
  • Interpretability in critical applications
  • Efficient and Practical.
  • Robust for diverse industries dealing with scalability, complex data, and limited resources.
